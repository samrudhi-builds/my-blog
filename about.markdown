### Hey there 👋

I'm **Samrudhi B**, a B.Tech Information Technology student with Honors in Cybersecurity at K.J. Somaiya College of Engineering, Mumbai.

I love fusing technical depth with design thinking. From blockchain innovation to secure systems and intuitive UI/UX, I build cross-functional solutions that actually make a difference.

What really excites me? Crafting secure digital futures at the intersection of **Cybersecurity**, **AI**, and **Blockchain**. I thrive on connecting diverse domains — from intuitive UI/UX design to robust smart contracts — to engineer bold, functional, and secure systems that solve real problems.

My passion shows up in award-winning hackathon solutions and internationally recognized prototypes. As an avid learner, I'm always curious, building, and seeking new scope for innovation.

#### Core Expertise

- **Cybersecurity** — Secure systems architecture, vulnerability analysis (XSS, SQL Injection), and blockchain forensics. Proficient with Burp Suite, Metasploit, DVWA, and Nmap.
- **Blockchain Development** — Smart contract development, decentralized systems, Bitcoin forensics (CADWAS tool at EUCLID Lab).
- **Full-Stack Development** — React, Node.js, Next.js, PHP/MySQL, Supabase, Tailwind CSS, Vercel.
- **Machine Learning** — Python-based ML models, data preprocessing, predictive analytics, exploratory data analysis.
- **UI/UX Design** — User-centered design, interaction prototyping, Figma. Certified via Coursera.

#### Writing & Research

I write about cybersecurity, containers, and building things at [Containers & Coffee](https://containersandcoffee.substack.com) and capture the world through a lens at [Frames & Frappes](https://framesandfrappes.substack.com). I also published a paper on food delivery app innovations at ICICCT-2025 in Springer.
